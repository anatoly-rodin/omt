Introducing OMToken

Our goal

Open Market Token team is creating a fee-less, instant, and custom-leveraged stock trading and securitization solution working in close integration with major stock exchanges. The ultimate goal of OMT is to overhaul online brokerage industry by merging the ingenuity of the crypto landscape with the international financial ecosystem and implementing blockchain protocols in the trading of physical financial assets. OMT tokenization framework allows investments to be more liquid and divisible among a greater number of people — an attribute that greatly reduces transaction costs and increases economies of scale. As a result, investors are exposed to more flexible and sophisticated investment instruments which can be tailored to meet the needs of investors allowing for increased accessibility to diversity, risk, return, and market exposure profiles.

Value proposition

OMToken is a next generation blockchain platform for investing and trading tokens which are linked to the shares traded on the major world stock exchanges or are valued digitally through investor designation — anything from securities, bonds, derivatives, or real estate may be represented; where prices can be determined and market participants can meet. An investor will use OMToken in his wallet to purchase shares and other security instruments structured as cryptocurrency creating a permanent record of a digital transaction immutable, secure and fast. OMT trade clearance is set to rout all retail flows to securities clearing houses directing trade orders through market makers.

how it works

1 STOCK TRADING

OMT is the world’s first fee-less decentralized stock trading platform built 100% on blockchain. Our built-in wallet fetches stock quotes from major stock exchanges and passes them to smart contract comparing the start price, end price, expiry price and reward winning traders immediately through smart contract. Additional database and server-side are required to process the trade data through clearing companies, centralized record holders and settling banks – per SEC regulations.

OMT trade clearance is to be set to rout all our retail flow to securities clearing brokers, by directing such orders to market making companies. A market maker makes a profit for itself and OMT by selling high and buying low. Market makers establish quotes whereby the bid price is set slightly lower than listed prices and the ask price is set slightly higher in order to earn a small margin. OMT is taking this one step further by offering users the opportunity to execute trades in our own Etherium blockchain fork, which increases security, execution speed and liquidity eventualy. The OMT trading application, is being developed for devices using iOS, Android or Windows Mobile operation systems is set to interact seamlessly with the Etherium blockchain ?fork?.

Unique tokenization solution

OMToken is using a new form securitization - the tokenization Profit Rights, also known as Profit Interest, which effectively is the right to receive a percentage of future profits from assets. With that OMToken has to attribute the following characteristics to the securitized asset for it to be fully implemented on the blockchain:

1 Proof of Authenticity

Our solution ensures the authenticity of assets using assets ownership and profit interest characteristics as a a legalized digital code, much like a serial number Assay/Certificate of Authenticity direct from the gold manufacturer, certificate of authenticity (COA) for artwork

2 Proof of Ownership

Our solution is a deterrent to fake ownership th anks to a Ricardian contract for issuance of the Profit Right is verified by digital signatures of the asset owners and their legal consultant before the asset can be put on our blockchain trading platform

3 Big data

Our solution provides market participants with business intelligence about origin and history of an asset during its life span which is stipulated in terms of Ricardian contract

4 Blockchain use viability

Our solution uses OMToken as a medium of exchange on our trading platform, providing security, liquidity and intrinsic investment value.

5 Fractional ownership

Our solution allows for the ownership of any asset to be broken into tiny parts for a liquidity purposes as Profit Right can be subdivided into even the tiniest interest fractions feasible.

6 Inexpensive tokenization process

Low to zero costs associated with insurance, auditing and third-party guarantees, custodian services and other legal burden. OMT tokenization model has had many of such costs totally eliminated or drastically reduced.
