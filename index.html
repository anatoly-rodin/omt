Introducing OMToken

Our goal

Open Market Token team is creating a fee-less, instant, and custom-leveraged stock trading and securitization solution working in close integration with major stock exchanges. The ultimate goal of OMT is to overhaul online brokerage industry by merging the ingenuity of the crypto landscape with the international financial ecosystem and implementing blockchain protocols in the trading of physical financial assets. As a result, investors are exposed to more flexible and sophisticated investment instruments which can be tailored to meet the needs of investors allowing for increased accessibility to diversity, risk, return, and market exposure profiles.

An investor will use OMToken in his wallet to purchase shares and other security instruments structured as cryptocurrency creating a permanent record of a digital transaction immutable, secure and fast. how it works - I

STOCK TRADING

OMT is the world’s first fee-less decentralized stock trading platform built 100% on blockchain. Our built-in wallet fetches stock quotes from major stock exchanges and passes them to smart contract comparing the start price, end price, expiry price and reward winning traders immediately through smart contract. Additional database and server-side are required to process the trade data through clearing companies, centralized record holders and settling banks – per SEC regulations.

OMT trade clearance is to be set to rout all our retail flow to securities clearing brokers, by directing such orders to market making companies. A market maker makes a profit for itself and OMT by selling high and buying low. Market makers establish quotes whereby the bid price is set slightly lower than listed prices and the ask price is set slightly higher in order to earn a small margin. OMT is taking this one step further by offering users the opportunity to execute trades in our own Etherium blockchain fork, which increases security, execution speed and liquidity eventualy. The OMT trading application, is being developed for devices using iOS, Android or Windows Mobile operation systems is set to interact seamlessly with the Etherium blockchain fork.

What is so unique about your Platform?
There are so many platforms up and running already?
Our unique features, which makes our product more attractive. are zero fee trading opportunity coupled with an innovative approach to tokenization of real assets based on Profit mechanism.
Why blockchain and How do you use Blockchain in your product?
We believe that blockchain technology brings and obvious advantages to the users of our platform. OMToken will attribute the following characteristics to the securitized asset for it to be fully implemented on the blockchain:
  • Proof of Authenticity - Our solution ensures the authenticity of assets using assets ownership and profit interest characteristics as a legalized digital code, much like a serial number Assay/Certificate of Authenticity direct from the gold manufacturer, certificate of authenticity (COA) for artwork.
  • Proof of Ownership - Our solution is a deterrent to fake ownership thanks to a Ricardian Contract for issuance of the Profit Right. The Contract is verified by digital signatures of the asset owners and their legal consultants before the asset can be put on our blockchain trading platform.
  • Big Data - Our solution provides market participants with business intelligence about origin and history of an asset during its life span which is stipulated in terms of Ricardian contract.
  • Blockchain Use Viability – Our solution uses OMToken as a medium of exchange on our trading platform, providing security, liquidity and intrinsic investment value.
  • Fractional Ownership – Our solution allows for the ownership of any asset to be broken into tiny parts for a liquidity purposes as Profit Right can be subdivided into even the tiniest interest fractions feasible.
  • Inexpensive Tokenization Process – low to zero costs associated with insurance, auditing and third-party guarantees, custodian services and other legal burden. OMT tokenization model has had many of such costs totally eliminated or drastically reduced.
What actual problems does OMToken solve?
Open Market Token is creating a universal, instant, custom-leveraged trading and investment solution that will work flawlessly with data flows integrated in major stock exchanges and with private investors from all over the world, processing trading data on the Ethereum blockchain leveraging smart contract technology.
